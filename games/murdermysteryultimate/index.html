Murder Mystery Ultimate

This is a  twist  on the classic murder mystery party game, where  zero preparation  is needed because the players construct the story collaboratively with the help of cues. Because of this,  nobody knows  who the murderer is until the end, so  no deception  skills are needed: it only takes deduction skills, memory and a bit of luck to win the game.

3-6 players

30-45 min

Age 12+
