Murder Mystery Ultimate

This is a twist on the classic murder mystery party game, where zero preparation is needed because the players construct the story collaboratively with the help of cues. Because of this, nobody knows who the murderer is until the end, so no deception skills are needed: it only takes deduction, memory and a bit of luck to win the game.

  • 3-6 players
  • Age 12yrs+
  • 30-45 min
