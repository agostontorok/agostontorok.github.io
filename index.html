My name is Ágoston Török. I'm a cognitive psychologist and a PhD candidate at the Eötvös Loránd University. I'm doing my research at the Brain Imaging Centre, RCNS, HAS. My interests are the brain dynamics of space perception and spatial cognition, multisensory integration and formerly the perception of prosody.
